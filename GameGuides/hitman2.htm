Tips and Tricks

Hitman 2016 in Hitman 2

If you own Hitman 2016 it is possible to play it in Hitman 2 with all its improvements (and the ability to use Ansel). Installing the Hitman 2 Starter Pack gives you access to the Hitman Lagecy pack.
